What is a Linear Regression?
We model: Outcome = β₀ + β₁·X₁ + β₂·X₂ + …  The slope β tells us how much the outcome changes per unit increase in each predictor. R² measures what proportion of variation in the outcome the model explains. In Phase 2 you will build your own model by dragging variables in — and observe what happens to R² as you add or remove them.
